Miry

Miriam (or Miry/Miry as everyone calls her) is a Spaniard from Madrid. An adventurous spirit, Miry moved to London when she was just 21, quickly learning how different our cultures can be! She now teaches English in Primary education and dedicates much of her time to her job, as she loves both teaching and English.

When she isn't working, Miry continues to dream of traveling and experiencing new cultures. While she has already visited many places since moving back to Madrid (including her No.1 destination: New York) her list of destinations to visits forever grows.

Fun fact: Miry always said she would never get married, and yet here we are!

Anthony

Anthony (or Ant, along with some other nicknames depending on who you ask) is an expat from the UK, now living in Madrid. Ant began his life in Spain much like many English expats, teaching English for very short stints. He now works for a internet/entertainment company doing the kind of job that is far too boring/complicated to explain.

Having studied photography in both college and university, Anthony continues to pursue photography, but now as a hobbie. Having worked as a photographer on a cruise ship, he very nearly killed the magic of photography for himself.

Fun fact: For a long while, Anthony was adamant in finding ways to shoot the wedding himself, fortunately Miry convinced him otherwise.

Our Story

Miry and Ant's story began on a boat (a cruise ship to be precise) and this particular one was called Ventura. In fact the first time Miry boarded Ventura, Ant was the second person she met, though at this point he had already spent a year travelling the high seas. While it was a big day for Miry (being her first day and all), it was the day Ant would buy his own camera after months of saving. To this day, this is how they both tell it.

Suffice to say, both Miry and Ant found that they were enjoying each others company more and more, with the next 6 months seeing them visit many places in the Caribbean and Mediterranean together.

When their contracts came to an end they each went to their seperate countries, staying in contact via Skype every night...
Of course, after having spent two weeks apart, Ant flew to Madrid to visit Miry and her family for the first time. Much to Miry's surprise, instead of taking his flight back, he remained in Spain and the pair have been together for the last six years or so!

One day during a pleasant trip to the mountains, despite his fear of heights, Ant proposed to Miry and she said yes!

Where is the wedding?

Aldea Tejera Negra

A small hamlet located in Guadalajara, Aldea Tejera Negra resides within the small town of Capillo de Ranas, one hour and forty-five minutes drive north of Madrid.

It is advised to rent a car for the occasion, or carpool with other guests, as the village is inaccessable via public transport.

If you have any concerns in arriving to the venue, we will try our best in helping you get here to celebrate our special day!

Aldea Tejera Negra

Situated in the mountains in the small village of Campillo de Ranas, the area is famed for its black slate-roof tiles. Despite having looked at numerous venues, Aldea Tejera Negra, struck a chord with us from the very beginning.

With the Autumn hues of red and yellow painting the landscape, the location may be remote and while the journey itself takes one hour and forty-five minutes by car from the center of Madrid, it stands as the perfect location to celebrate our wedding!
